E-Waste Awareness

Learn about the impact of e-waste and how to manage it safely:

  • E-waste contains toxic materials like lead, mercury, and cadmium.
  • Recycle to recover valuable metals like copper and gold.
  • Don’t burn e-waste – it releases harmful fumes.
  • Proper disposal protects soil, water, and air quality.
