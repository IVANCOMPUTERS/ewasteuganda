About Us

E-Waste Uganda is a community-driven initiative promoting responsible e-waste management through collection, recycling, and awareness.

Our Goals

  • Reduce e-waste pollution in Uganda
  • Promote recycling and safe disposal
  • Educate communities on e-waste impact
